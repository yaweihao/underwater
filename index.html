Our Story

I fell in love with water the summer I turned 21, while teaching kids in Chengdu. One kicked five meters. Another let go of the wall. A third swam the full length. I don’t remember the drills, but I remember their faces—wide, wild, relieved.

Life kept rushing—grad school, a tech career, endless screens. I kept swimming, yet missed the kind of connection coaching demands. When you teach kids you celebrate out loud, you cheer every breath, you stay fully present.

In 2025 I started Unplug Underwater. It isn’t a startup. It’s a quiet space to share the calm confidence that comes from a good lap, a deep breath, and the simple joy of moving through water.
